COOKING METHODS: TEMPERATURES, TIMES & CONVERSIONS
====================================================

This guide covers heat conversion, cooking time adjustments, and technique notes for all common kitchen equipment.

Fan-assisted (convection) oven: Reduce temperature by 20°C (35°F) or reduce time by 25%.

== AIR FRYER CONVERSIONS ==
Air fryers use rapid circulating hot air. General rules:
- Reduce conventional oven temperature by 15–20°C (25–30°F)
- Reduce cooking time by 20–25%
- Preheat for 3–5 minutes unless recipe says otherwise
- Shake basket or flip halfway through cooking
- Do NOT overcrowd — leave space for air circulation

== STOVETOP HEAT LEVELS ==
Setting       | Temperature | Use case
Low           | 65–95°C     | Melt chocolate, keep sauce warm, steep
Medium-Low    | 95–120°C    | Simmer, gentle sauces, scrambled eggs
Medium        | 120–160°C   | Sauté vegetables, cook onions, pancakes
Medium-High   | 160–200°C   | Sear, stir-fry, boil water faster
High          | 200–260°C   | Deep fry, wok cooking, initial boil

Sauté vs. fry vs. sear:
- Sauté: Medium heat, small amount of fat, frequent stirring
- Pan-fry: Medium-high heat, generous fat (shallow), occasional turning
- Sear: High heat, minimal fat, quick browning to develop Maillard crust

== MICROWAVE COOKING ==
Power levels: 100% (High) = 700–1200W depending on model.

Food                          | Power  | Time per 500g | Notes
Vegetables (frozen)           | High   | 4–5 min       | Covered, stir halfway
Fresh vegetables (steamed)    | High   | 3–4 min       | Add 2 tbsp water, covered
Fish fillets                  | Medium | 4–6 min       | Covered, rest 1 min
Chicken (pre-cooked, reheat)  | Medium | 2–3 min       | Cover, rest 1 min
Rice (cooked, reheat)         | High   | 1–2 min       | Add 1 tbsp water, covered
Soup                          | High   | 2–3 min       | Stir halfway
Poached eggs                  | Medium | 1–1.5 min     | Crack into water, cover
Scrambled eggs (2 eggs)       | High   | 1.5–2 min     | Stir at 30s intervals
Chocolate (melt)              | Low    | 1–2 min       | Stir every 30s
Butter (soften 100g)          | Low    | 20–30 s       | Watch carefully

Microwave tips:
- Arrange dense items at edge of plate, lighter items in centre
- Pierce potato/squash skin before microwaving to prevent explosion
- Cover food with microwave-safe lid or damp paper towel to retain moisture
- Rotate/stir halfway for even heating

== DEEP FRYING ==

Frying temperature guide:
160–170°C: Cakes, doughnuts, soft items
180°C: Chips/fries, breaded chicken, fish
190°C: Quick-fry items, churros, fritters

Test temperature without thermometer: Drop a small piece of bread — golden in 30 seconds = ~180°C.
