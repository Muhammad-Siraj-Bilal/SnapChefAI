# Hi Grandma! 👵

Since you asked about what I've been building, here is the simple version of **SnapChef AI**.

### 1. What is it? (The Concept)
Think of this like a digital kitchen assistant that's always in your pocket. You just take a picture of whatever is in your fridge or a photo of a meal you like, and the app instantly tells you exactly how to cook it. If you need it for more people, or want to swap out an ingredient because you ran out, you just ask it like you're talking to a friend, and it updates the recipe for you.

### 2. The "Smart" Bit (The AI)
Instead of just searching through a dusty old cookbook, this app has a "brain." It can actually **look** at your photos to see what vegetables you have. Then, it uses all its knowledge to **write** a new recipe just for you on the spot. It's smart enough to know if you're allergic to nuts or if you want to eat healthier, and it automatically changes the instructions so you don't even have to think about it!

### 3. How it's made (The "Engines")
To make this work, I used a few different parts:
*   **The Face:** A beautiful website that's easy to tap on and use.
*   **The Brain:** A very clever computer program that does the thinking and talking.
*   **The Memory:** A private library I built where the app stores all the best cooking tips so it never forgets a safe substitution.
*   **The Searcher:** It can even check the internet in real-time to find the best alternative if you're missing an egg or garlic!

It's like having a master chef and a nutritionist living in your phone! Love you! ❤️
